About Convergence

Join us at Convergence EMEA, the premier event for the Microsoft Dynamics community in Europe, the Middle East, and Africa--where opportunity and innovation meet to deliver real business value. It’s the chance to make key business connections and to discover the full potential of your Microsoft Dynamics solution. Past attendees agree, there’s no better way to get energized and network with others like you.

Convergence 2013 EMEA
4—6 November 2013
Barcelona, Spain
Fira Barcelona Gran Via Conference Centre

What’s New at Convergence 2013 EMEA? Convergence EMEA will feature important product announcements across Microsoft Dynamics NAV, AX, and CRM. You’ll be hearing from the best- informed experts speaking about the latest enhancements to the products you use every day.

Microsoft Dynamics CRM 2013 helps make business personal by delivering a reimagined user experience, new mobile capabilities including sales applications for Windows 8 tablets and iPads, and embedded social capabilities to keep users connected to the people and resources they need.

Microsoft Dynamics NAV 2013 R2 features new cash management, hosting on Windows Azure Infrastructure Services, and new business opportunities with Microsoft Office 365.

Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 features new Windows 8 business apps, industry-specific enhancements, and the brand new Lifecycle Services that help customers like you get even more value from Microsoft.

And to help you gain a deeper understanding of the direction and roadmap of the future of Microsoft Dynamics, Tuesday’s keynote will feature Microsoft executive Kirill Tatarinov, executive vice president, Microsoft Business Solutions Group, who will present the latest perspectives on the advancement of business solutions into the cloud.

Be sure to allocate plenty of time to visit the new Convergence Experience Center, where you will experience one-on-one product demonstrations and networking opportunities. In addition, the Convergence Expo will feature many of the top Microsoft Dynamics independent software vendors (ISVs) and exhibitors who offer add-on solutions to help you and your organization run more productively.

Don’t miss your chance to connect with your Microsoft Dynamics User Group community on the opening Monday of the conference as they host a series of informative breakout sessions. Hosted by users, for users, these user group sessions provide you the opportunity to share your experiences and learn from other Microsoft Dynamics customers just like you.
